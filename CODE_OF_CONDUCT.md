# Code of Conduct

Dimly is an open and welcoming project.  
Everyone contributing is expected to help maintain a respectful, inclusive, and collaborative environment.

---

## Our Standards

We are committed to making participation in this project a positive experience for everyone. Examples of behavior that contributes to a healthy environment include:

- Being respectful and considerate in discussions.  
- Welcoming constructive feedback and different viewpoints.  
- Focusing on what’s best for users and the project.  
- Showing empathy toward other community members.

Unacceptable behaviors include:

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks.  
- Demeaning or disrespectful comments.  
- Publishing others’ private information without consent.  
- Any conduct that could reasonably be considered inappropriate.

---

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all project spaces, including issues, pull requests, and community channels, as well as when representing the project in public.

---

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported by contacting the maintainer at  
**[GitHub profile](https://github.com/Brian-S-Poon)**.

All reports will be reviewed and handled in a fair and timely manner.  
Maintainers reserve the right to remove, edit, or reject contributions that violate this Code.
